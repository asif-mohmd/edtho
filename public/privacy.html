Privacy Policy

At Edutho, we value your privacy. Edutho is a secure, no-login online notepad designed for creating and sharing private notes or code snippets. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. No Account Required

You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use Edutho. All notes are stored anonymously.

2. Data Handling

- Notes are saved temporarily and can be accessed via unguessable URLs.
- If you set a password for a note, it is hashed securely before storage.
- Empty or expired notes may be deleted automatically by our system.

3. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand usage patterns and improve the platform. No personally identifiable information is collected or stored.

4. No Cookies

Edutho does not use cookies for tracking or marketing purposes.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your notes or content with third parties. Only the people you share your unique note link with can view the content.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect data, including encryption and periodic cleanup of unused notes.

7. Children’s Privacy

Edutho is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with the date of revision.
